Margaret Eloise Knight

"My Favorite Bag Lady"

  • 1838: Born February 14, 1838 in York, Maine
  • 1850: Developed safety device that turned a machine off if it malfunctioned
  • 1870: Founded Eastern Paper Bag Company
  • 1871: Filed patent interference suit against Charles Annan for her paper bag invention obtaining her first patent
  • 1879: Made further improvements to the paper bag machine
  • 1880: Developed three domestic items
  • 1883: Designed a dress and skirt shield
  • 1884: Developed a clasp for holding robes
  • 1885: Designed a spit, a tool used to skewer meat for cooking
  • 1890: Designed several machines that improved the process of cutting and sewing shoes.
  • 1890-1894: Acquired six patents for shoe factory machines
  • 1902-1914: Patented a series of inventions related to rotary engines such as components for rotary engines and motors
  • 1914: Died October 12, 1914 (aged 76) Framingham, Massachusetts
